The new Nothing

The Nothing Phone (2) looks similar to its predecessor in terms of design but comes with improvements in almost all aspects -- performance, display, battery, charging speeds, software, etc. The much-hyped Glyph LEDs have been upgraded too, both in terms of hardware and software, and are now more customisable, functional and usable. The Glyph UI also features third-party app integration with services like Uber and Zomato, and hopefully the brand will add more functionality going forward. The Phone (2) seems priced on the higher side if pure specs are taken into consideration, and the camera could do with a few improvements, but if the Glyph UI and the design appeals to you, it could prove to be a worthy buy.
